About Us

Welcome to Mega City Cab, your trusted partner in safe, reliable, and efficient transportation. We are committed to providing a seamless ride-hailing experience, ensuring comfort, affordability, and convenience for our customers. Whether you need a quick trip across the city or a long-distance ride, our professional drivers and well-maintained vehicles are always ready to serve you.

At Mega City Cab, we prioritize customer satisfaction, safety, and punctuality. Our easy-to-use web application allows users to book rides effortlessly, track ride history, and manage payments—all in one place.

Our History

Founded in 2020, Mega City Cab started as a small fleet of taxis catering to local commuters in Colombo. Over the years, we expanded our services, embracing technology to provide a more efficient and customer-friendly experience.

  • 2020 – Established our first fleet of taxis in Colombo.
  • 2022 – Expanded to cover more areas across Sri Lanka.
  • 2025 – Launched our online booking system to streamline ride management.

Today, we continue to innovate and improve, ensuring that our customers receive the best cab service possible.
